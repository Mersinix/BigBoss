====================================================
PART 3 - APPROVAL FLOW
======================

Supplier creates product
↓
Status = PENDING
↓
Appears in:
Admin > Products > Supplier Products
↓
Admin reviews
↓
Approve
↓
Status = ACTIVE
↓
Product moves automatically to:
Admin > Product Catalog
↓
Product becomes available inside:
Supplier > Products > Admin Products

After approval:

* Product disappears from Supplier Products queue.
* Product disappears from supplier pending list.
* Product becomes part of approved catalog.
