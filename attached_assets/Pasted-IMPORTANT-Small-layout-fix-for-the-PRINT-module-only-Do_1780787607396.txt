* The hero banner scrolls under / behind the navbar
* As a result, the category flatlist + filters section becomes visually too far from the navbar
* This creates an empty visual gap at the top of the page after the banner scrolls away

---

## GOAL

Ensure that once the hero banner scrolls out of view:

* The flatlist (category/service selector)
* The filters section

stay visually “attached” / close to the navbar area.

---

## SOLUTION (UI ONLY)

Apply one of the following existing-layout-safe approaches:

1. Adjust top spacing of the first content section (flatlist container) so that:

   * It reduces unnecessary top margin/padding after banner scroll

2. OR make the filters + flatlist container behave as a “sticky-aligned block” under the navbar after hero scroll

3. OR add conditional spacing:

   * When hero is visible → normal spacing

Use existing layout system only.
