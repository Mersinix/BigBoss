Variant display

Improve how variants are displayed in the Pack Products page.

Currently, if one product contains two variants, each variant is expanded into one line per flavor, for example:

Oatly Barista 6x1L Pack

Vanilla · Espresso Shot — $22.00 · 22 in stock
Caramel · Espresso Shot — $22.00 · 22 in stock
Hazelnut · Espresso Shot — $22.00 · 22 in stock

Hazelnut · Small — $33.00 · 33 in stock
Unflavored · Small — $33.00 · 33 in stock

Instead, variants should remain grouped.

Example:

Oatly Barista 6x1L Pack

( Vanilla • Caramel • Hazelnut ) · Espresso Shot — $22.00 · 22 in stock

( Unflavored • Hazelnut ) · Small — $33.00 · 33 in stock

The supplier should work with variants, not individual flavors.

Each variant should display:

Variant size
Available flavors grouped together
Price
Available stock

When creating a Pack, the supplier should choose the variant and specify the quantity for that variant only.

Later, when the Coffee Owner purchases the Pack, they will distribute that quantity between the available flavors belonging to the selected variant.

This keeps the supplier workflow simple while still allowing the Coffee Owner to customize flavor distribution.

Pack creation rule

Update the Pack creation requirement.

Currently the workflow requires at least two products before creating a Pack.

Replace this rule.

A Pack should require at least two selected variants, regardless of which products they belong to.

Examples:

Product A → Variant 1 + Variant 2 → ✅ Allowed
Product A → Variant 1 + Product B → Variant 1 → ✅ Allowed
Product A → Variant 1 + Product B → Variant 2 → ✅ Allowed
Only one selected variant → ❌ Not allowed

The minimum requirement is two variants, not two different products.

Everything else in the Pack creation workflow should remain unchanged.
